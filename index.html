About Vikram University

Established in 1957, Vikram University is one of the leading institutions located in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh. The university is committed to academic excellence, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Its rich history, modern infrastructure, and highly qualified faculty make it a center of learning and innovation.
